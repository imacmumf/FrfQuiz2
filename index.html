Financial Manager Assessment


Could Your Financial Manager Be Cheating You?


This assessment is meant for people who rely on an advisor or manager to manage their cash and finances. In the assessment, we refer to this person as a "financial manager". Although this person may also handle your investments, this assessment does not cover investment advice or management.


This assessment is intended to identify whether certain behaviors frequently associated with losses due to a financial manager exist in your financial affairs. It does not guarantee it will identify all risks that might cause you a loss, whether caused by your financial manager or not; nor does the presence of some risk factors necessarily mean you should drop your financial manager. Where risks are identified, you would be prudent to examine them more closely and take steps to reduce your exposure.


This assessment is intended to identify whether certain behaviors frequently associated with losses due to unacceptable or improper behavior by an investment advisor exist in your investment advisory relationship. It does not guarantee it will identify all risks that might cause you a loss, whether caused by your investment advisor or not; nor does the presence of some risk factors necessarily mean you should drop your advisor. Where risks are identified, you would be prudent to examine them more closely and take steps to reduce your exposure.


On the other hand, if your assessment does not identify high levels of risk, it doesn't mean you don't have any risk or that you won't have future losses. This doesn't cover every behavior or risk factor that might exist in an untrustworthy advisor. In addition, there are many other areas where your finances can be at risk without any unacceptable or improper behavior by your advisor.


In this Assessment, the term "financial manager" refers to people and/or their firms who do any of the following: manage your finances, handle your banking, negotiate contracts, provide financial advice or pay your bills. You may know them as an "Agent", "Financial or Business Manager", "Financial Planner", "Wealth Manager", or another similar title.


Please answer all of the following questions. The questions are about your advisor, not about your knowledge of finances or investing. Your answers should be based on your understanding of the questions and best recollection.


When you complete the Assessment and submit your answers, you will receive a report identifying your risk score and an interpretation of that score, as well as high level suggestions for follow up. If you have high risk scores in one or more areas, detailed discussion of your answers and specific follow up is available.
